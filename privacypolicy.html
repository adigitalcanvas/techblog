Privacy Notice

1. Data Controller

This website is operated by the site owner (portfolio holder). For any privacy-related queries, you may contact the owner via the contact details provided on this website.

2. Purpose of Processing

We use privacy-focused analytics to understand how visitors interact with the site, improve content, and demonstrate professional work to potential employers and collaborators.

3. Analytics Provider

This website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first analytics service that does not rely on cookies and does not track users across websites.

4. Data Collected

The analytics service processes limited, non-identifying technical data, which may include:

  • Aggregated page views and visit duration
  • General geographic region (not precise location)
  • Device, browser, and referring website

No personally identifiable information (PII) is intentionally collected.

5. Lawful Basis (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Processing is based on legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR), specifically to:

  • Maintain and improve the website
  • Understand general audience engagement

This is balanced against your rights, as the data collected is minimal, anonymized/aggregated, and non-intrusive.

6. Cookies

This website does not use cookies for analytics purposes.

7. Data Sharing and Transfers

Analytics data is processed by Cloudflare, which may involve data processing outside your country of residence. Appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained only in aggregated form for a limited period necessary to analyze website performance.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., EU/UK), you may have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request erasure or restriction
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest

Given the non-identifying nature of the data, exercising some of these rights may be limited.

10. Changes to This Notice

This privacy notice may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the website indicates acceptance of the current version.
